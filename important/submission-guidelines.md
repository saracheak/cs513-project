# Submission Guidelines

You may zip your project into a single file and submit it here. Only one submission per team is required. Presentations will be scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis. Please bring two printed copies of your slides at the time of your presentation.

Your submission should include:

- The **name of the project** on the first page of the PowerPoint presentation.
- The **names and photos of all presenters** on the first page of the PowerPoint presentation.

## Project/Case Study description:   

### Project:

Prepare and present a real-world data mining project including:

- Problem Statement
- Data
- Methodology(s)/Algorithm(s)
- Software
- Execution and Analysis
- References and Documentation

### Project Rubric/ Performance Measurements:

- Novelty of the project idea(s)
- Techniques used
- Comparison of the results of the above techniques applied to the data
- Uniqueness of the data source(s)
- Additional techniques extending those studied in the class
- Quality of the presentation material and presentations
- Timing/Sequence of the presentation

Additional credit for Agentic Models
